Most people sitting on Finnish company boards have just one board seat

There are a total of 1346 board seats in Finnish listed companies. There are some indivuduals who have power in multiple companies: 1.11 percent of all board members have five board seats. The average number of board seats per person is 1.18. Allmost 20 percent of those who are board members in finnish public companies sit in two different boards.

Eight percent of board members form a network of power

A square represents one board seat. By hovering the cursor over a square, you can see a name of a board member

The figures are derived from the companies' annual reports 2023-2024. Data was collected 4/2024 by Alma Business Media.
